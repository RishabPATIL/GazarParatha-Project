The ingredients to make gazarparatha is:
     1 : 1 Grated Carrot
     2 : 100 gm Whole Wheat Flour
     3 : 1/4 tsp Red Chilli Powder
     4 : 1/4 tsp Cummin Seeds Powder
     5 : 1/2 tsp salt
     6 : 1/3 cup water
     7 : 1 tsp oil or ghee
     8 : extra oil or ghee for cooking the parathas
